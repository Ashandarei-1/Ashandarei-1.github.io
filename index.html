Tournament Rules

Below are the rules and guidelines for participating in our tournaments.

  • Matches are to be played in conquest mode. The first player to win 2 of 3 matches wins the showdown.
  • There are no restrictions on card usages. Players may use a different deck between matches. Matches do not need to be played on the same day as long as they are all completed within the month.
  • Players are to complete each showdown within the month shown on the schedule at a time(s) agreed upon by both players.
  • Players will submit the results of each match and any replays after the showdown so the standings can be updated accordingly.
  • After each month of showdowns the top 2 players from each group (A,B,C) will move on to the next round until the final showdown.
  • The final showdown will be a best of 5.
